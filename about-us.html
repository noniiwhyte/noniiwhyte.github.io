OUR VISION

"Having worked with public and private sector international groups both in the Upstream and Downstream sectors of the Oil & Gas industry over the years, we have become adept at bridging the energy requirements of groups with an eye on Africa. We have been able to carefully deduce, detail and deliver on the needs of our strategic partners operating in the African region. This has helped us define the vision for our mission; to be one of the leading energy companies in Africa” – C.E.O.

- OUR GROUP -

Subsidiaries/units that contribute to Webb Energy's core business, making it possible for us to effectively offer a broad range of products and services.

WEBB ENERGY EXPLORATION AND PRODUCTION

We provide market intelligence and logistics services to Oil companies operating in the region, value added analysis and contact interfaces for companies seeking opportunities in the Oil & Gas industry.
Our efficient geological and geophysical team puts us in a firm position to assist companies in their quest for hydrocarbons in oil-rich African regions

WEBB ENERGY OIL SERVICES

Webb Energy Oil Services Limited is a value-driven company providing quality equipment rentals and services to the E&P and Service companies in the upstream sector of the Nigerian Oil and Gas Industry.

WEBB ENERGY GAS AND LOGISTICS

Webb Energy Gas and Logistics provides a full range of onshore and offshore services, customized logistics solutions for specific needs and turnkey solutions 24/7 ranging from supply chain management of rigs and FPSO vessels through to the transportation of heavy-lift structures.
All these are supported by our in-house transport engineering division.

AGSN

AGSN is a subsidiary of Webb Energy, dealing in Oil and Gas Trading, Terminals and Oil Services, dedicated to providing top quality service delivery as well as building internal local capacity through the training of local staff force.
