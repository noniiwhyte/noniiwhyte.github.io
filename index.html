- WHO ARE WE -

Webb Energy is an integrated Oil, Gas and Power Company involved in Oil & Gas Trading, Oil Services and Upstream activities. 

Incorporated on the 1st of December 1998, Webb Energy has evolved into a full multi-disciplinary group working on various contracts in the Oil and Gas Sector from Upstream, Midstream and Downstream in Nigeria, standing out with top international resource companies operating in the Gulf of Guinea region through first class service delivery.

- ABOUT US -

OUR VISION

"Having worked with public and private sector international groups both in the Upstream and Downstream sectors of the Oil & Gas industry over the years, we have become adept at bridging the energy requirements of groups with an eye on Africa. We have been able to carefully deduce, detail and deliver on the needs of our strategic partners operating in the African region. This has helped us define the vision for our mission; to be one of the leading energy companies in Africa” – C.E.O
