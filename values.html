HEALTH, SAFETY & ENVIRONMENT

Here at Webb Energy, SAFETY is core to our work culture. We set strict policies committed to ensure a safe work environment each and every day. We aim at achieving excellence in our Health, Safety and Environment performances and to foster continuous improvement in every aspect of our business.

EMPLOYEE SAFETY

Our HSE policies enlighten employees to stop work believed to adversely impact the health and safety of others or the environment as well as report potentially unsafe conditions. This encourages proactive monitoring to prevent the imminence of a potential incident.

CONTRACTOR SAFETY

We are committed to ensuring that our contractors and suppliers comply with our HSE standard and policies, which includes a comprehensive contractor management and safety program. Our contractors complete a prequalification process including a safety orientation and training before arriving at any company site.
