- SERVICES & ACTIVITIES -

OIL & GAS TRADING

Webb Energy has been involved in Oil and Gas trading for close to two decades and have acquired prowess in oil delivery in and out of the African continent, trading cargoes of crude oil in excess of 160kbd out of Nigeria over the last couple of years as well as fuel oil and other products. Currently we have a joint 30,000 barrel a day Crude Oil contract with South African Strategic Fuel Fund (SSF) being supplied to the South African Government. We are currently setting up a logistics solution centre to deliver 30,000 MT of PMS into Nigeria on a revolving month to month basis. We also continue to supply the Nigerian National Petroleum Corporation with PMS through long standing relationships with a number of established European trading houses.

REFINING

Webb Energy, through Portofino Asset Corporation, was part of the Management and Board of Directors that ran the Sogara Refinery designed to “top” mandji crude as well as supply distillates to the Gabonese market and export naphtha and fuel oil. In the early days, Elf Gabon built 80,000 cubic meters fuel storage units at Cap Lopez 5 km from Port Gentil at the crude export terminal. Sogara Refinery always processed 100% 28 API mandji crude that comes by pipeline from the largest oil production field in Gabon. The heavy crude produces a high fuel oil yield, most of which is exported and earns the government foreign currency.

MARINE SERVICES

Webb Energy has transitioned into instituting a strong logistics support and services arm given the civil unrest in the Niger Delta region. We develop a system of supporting our exploration and production partners in the following areas:
• Off shore support and supply vessel and this includes 6,000HP, sea going barges/crane (200t) and Barges
• Professional marine engineers and deep sea divers that are trained to handle difficult marine equipment and logistics.
• Contracting technical and engineering support to plants piping & vessels for all plants.
• Production of lighter vessels for evacuation of petroleum products

STORAGE & TERMINAL MANAGEMENT

Currently Webb Energy plans to build a prime facility with a storage capacity of 45000 metric tonnes in addition to floating strategic storage vessels with operations scheduled to start in 2016.

POWER
